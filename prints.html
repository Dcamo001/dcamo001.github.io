Anything on this website or in my instagram can be printed, delivery usually within 2-3 weeks. Every print is custom made and hand crafted to your custom size. It is then inspected for craftsmanship, quality, and color, My printing partner and I take a lot of pride in what we do and want to make sure that you are 100% satisfied with the end product.

Please note that photos under the 'Instagram' tab may not yet be published on my website, but are available for print. If you would like one of those photos printed and can't find it in my albums, please email me to let me know which photo you would like!

NOTE: I donate 5% of all print sale profits to organizations which focus on social and environmental issues. If you have a prefered organization that you like to donate to, feel free to let me know where the funds should be directed either through the comment box when you are checking out, or through email/direct message.

Here are my preferred printing methods:

Printed on metal. Photos are heat infused directly onto Chromaluxe brand aluminum sheets. These are glossy, sharp, and vivid - great for high resolution printing, and resists fading for 65+ years. Standard mount option is a .5" float mount.

8" x 10" -- $60
12" x 18" -- $120
16" x 24" -- $200
20" x 30" -- $300
30" x 45" -- $600

Printed on canvas. Photos are printed onto 400gsm white poly-cotton blend specifically designed for photography. Background is bright white which makes for a vibrant print. Can be made with a .75" or 1.5" inch stretched wrap, and either the image itself can wrap over the sides, or you can choose a solid color for the wrap. Please let me know what you'd prefer in the same box below that you provide the file number.

8" x 10" -- $60
12" x 18" -- $120
16" x 24" -- $200
20" x 30" -- $300
30" x 45" -- $600

Photo paper. Option between glossy white or glossy metallic photo paper. Both options are heavyweight, high-gloss for maximum color and image resolution. The metallic photo paper displays an additional level of iridescence and depth, especially in different lighting situations. Both options are high quality and cost effective.

8" x 10" -- $25
12" x 18" -- $40
16" x 24" -- $75
20" x 30" -- $100
30" x 45" -- $150

These are the most common sizes and prices that I print at. If you're interested in a custom size, please send me a message and we'll set it up!
