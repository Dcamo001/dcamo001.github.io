Fly fishing - Bad Medicine Lake, Montana


I am a Berkeley, CA based photo/videographer.

I grew up in Herndon, Virginia, and spent much of my time outdoors exploring the woods and rivers surrounding my neighborhood which developed a love of adventure, nature, and exploration from a young age. My interest in photography started when I received my first film camera as a gift from grandparents just before a trip to Europe, when I was about 8 years old. I discovered that one of my passions is capturing and sharing the world as I see it in my mind.

Since then, and after moving from Northern Virginia to California, I spend nearly every weekend shooting all up and down the West Coast, focusing on landscapes, portraits, events, product, real estate, and more.

If you'd like to work together, send me a message!

All content within this site is my own. I edit my work using Adobe Lightroom, Photoshop, and Davinci Resolve.
