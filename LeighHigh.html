Leigh High School Class of 2020 Graduation


The Leigh High School (San Jose, CA) graduating class of 2020 had a unique end of school year
and graduation ceremony experience, being affected by the global Coronavirus pandemic.

The academic year concluded with distanced, online learning for the last several months of the
school year. Face masks were mandatory while crossing the graduation stage, with golf
carts ferrying students with their families one by one between the parking lot and the stage.

Congratulations to Leigh High School Graduating Class of 2020!
